Tyler, The Creator

2009

Tyler The Creator's debut mixtape dropped in 2009. With tracks like "Seven" and "French", Tyler created extreme controversy With his graphic and vulgar lyricism.

2011

Goblin was Tyler's first official album, under his new manager Clancy. Breakout tracks like "Yonkers" brought the alternative rapper into the spotlight, once more an object of criticism for his unusually dark lyrics.
